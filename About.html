About Lexicon Language School

Lexicon Language School was established in Cork, Ireland in 1990 by Andrew Lennox, the president of the school, following a successful career within the further education sector. Andrew sets high standards and travels the world to promote the school to new students and agents. Since 1990, Lexicon Language School has grown into a truly international school, helping many students from South America and Asia to improve their English language skills. We have developed an excellent reputation for providing quality English language tuition in a relaxed and welcoming environment. Every year, we help hundreds of students improve their English language skills and achieve their academic or career goals.

Many of our students come back to study at the school, or send their friends and family. Our number of personal recommendations is very high.

We offer a range of courses year round, and you can study from short courses to one year or more. You can also participate in regular social and cultural events.

Whatever your requirements, our friendly staff are here to assist you and ensure that you enjoy your stay in Cork city.
